ABOUT US

Active Transport | SYNTAX is a website made by four STEM IT student developers with a finals PT for Practical Research 2 that needed to be accomplished. Through cramming and collaboration, with a side of chaos and love, we were able to accomplish this feat and bring you an informative website all about Active Transport!

Find out more about us down below!

OUR MISSION
Quality content on Active Transport.

We provide learning materials on the topic of Active Transport for students to use freely. We aim to make learning about of Active Transport more accessible and digestable. To be a trust-worthy source of information and resources for students.

OUR VISION
Expansion & Growth.

We strive to expand SYNTAX into a proper learning hub for students. Exapnding the content and scope of the website to include other Biology 1 lessons to further assist students' learning. The SYNTAX team envisions a website full of resources to aid students and help them easily access and understand their lessons.

OUR VALUES
Education is key.

We believe that education plays a vital role in the development of all students in achieving their full potential. We condone the active search for knowledge and learning. We value inclusivity and understanding above all else.
